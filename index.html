📚 How to get a Channel ID

Method 1: Forward any message from your channel to @userinfobot — it replies with the chat ID.

Method 2: From a t.me/c/1234567890/42 link → channel ID is -1001234567890 (prepend -100).

Method 3: Visit https://api.telegram.org/bot<TOKEN>/getUpdates in your browser after sending a message in your channel.

⚠️ Your bot must be added as administrator in the channel with Post messages and Delete messages rights.
